Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What is the need for data preprocessing ?

    A: Data preprocessing is an integral step in Machine Learning as the quality of data and the useful information that can be derived from it directly affects the ability of our model to learn; therefore, it is extremely important that we preprocess our data before feeding it into our model.

  • Q. Can I get an image dataset preprocessed?

    A. Yes, we accept images, text, tar, JSON, CSV, XSLS, videos type datasets.

  • Q. Do I need a paid subscription?

    A. No, all you need to sign up and you can start using our services.

  • Q. In which format do I get the result?

    A. You get the preprocessed data in CSV format after the process is complete along with a log file.

  • Q. If my input is in video format, will I get the output in video format as well?

    A. No, you get the preprocessed data in CSV format as output.
